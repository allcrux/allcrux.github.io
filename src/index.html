    Dynamic Structure Charts

    Carve out relevant entities from the overall universe. Use smart and predefined views with highlighting features to focus on what is relevant. Calculate ultimate beneficial ownerships.

    Tax & Legal Reporting Automation

    Automatically create PDF reports from the data under control. Further automation may take the form of automated submission of withholding tax returns or preparing (part of) the full corporate income tax return package.

    One-Click Due Diligence

    Replicate all data embedded in a structure, select what needs to be made available and export it to various formats for easy upload into any due diligence process.

    Back-to-the-future

    The Time Slider allows to recreate the status of a specific investment structure on a given date. With the Corpware Simulator you can extrapolate into the future and get a forward looking view of the behavior of a specific structure.

    Transfer Pricing (TP) Support

    Generate TP-reports on the basis of predefined templates and have certain key data (and benchmarks) filled in automatically.

    Fatca/CRS Support

    Fatca and CRS processes require listing all equity and debt claim holders for reporting entities (and controlling persons for passives). Reprt beneficial owners and debt claim holders. All relevant US forms and self-certifications can be automatically populated on the basis of relevant data stored in the database.
